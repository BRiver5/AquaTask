Short description: Track water daily. Offline goals, history charts, reminders. No login.

Long description:
AquaTask helps you stay hydrated without stress, accounts, or ads.

Key features:
• One-tap water logging — presets (100–300 ml) or custom amount
• Animated daily progress ring toward your personal goal
• History: daily, weekly, and monthly charts
• Works fully offline — log drinks anytime, sync when online
• Optional local drink reminders (start/end time, interval)
• Personalization: theme color and drink icon
• No registration, no ads, no third-party trackers

How it works: open the app, tap how much you drank, and watch your progress grow. Set a daily goal, review your history, and build a simple hydration habit at your own pace.
